What do you need help with?

Can I track my order?

We rely on a global network of shipping services (UPS, FedEx, DHL) to get your order to your doorstep as soon as possible. For this reason, tracking is not always available.

I want to change my order.

Because each product on KIWI is made to order we canâ€™t make changes to orders that have been finalized and shipped. However, if your order has shipped, not to worry. You can still return it for free and get what you want instead.

Return After Purchasing

Returns can be submitted within 90 days after receiving your order for most items. Returns for masks can be submitted within 14 days of receipt.

Do you accept cash on delivery?

At the moment we don't offer cash on delivery or bank transfer as payment methods. If you don't own a credit card, we suggest you use PayPal or Sofort (if available in your country), as they only require your bank information.

Do you offer gift wrapping?

No at this current time.

Will my poster or art print be folded or creased?

No. Posters and art prints are rolled into sturdy rectangular boxes, while framed art prints are shipped in fitted boxes.
